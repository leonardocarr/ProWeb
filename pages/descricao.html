Sobre as nossas APIS

We have invested in the development of a set of APIs that will allow our partners and third-party providers to connect to our systems.

Leveraging this technology, we are offering integration capabilities for functionalities such as Quote & Buy, Claims creation and management and Expert Orders across three major business lines: Motor, Health and Household.

This will allow our products to become accessible across companies and platforms, through external services and applications, banking agency branch or self-service client applications, brokers' own channels and more.
